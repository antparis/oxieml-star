# FINDINGS 2026-06-14 -- [CONJECTURE] Modular weight (dose of non-holomorphy) and the structure of neutrino generations.

## Origin
Emerged from a conceptual session (Anthony's intuition) at the close of the eclectic Delta(54)
study, after the door/lock images: mirror-door (both sides identical = real-trapped, rejected),
empty-door (no structure, trivial), chiral-door (lock one side only, irreducible = the genuine
target). Anthony's framing: maybe the GEOMETRY (how much non-holomorphic content a state carries)
underlies the generation structure, with mass being a consequence rather than the cause.

## Statement (precise, survivable version) -- [CONJECTURE]
NOT "the three flavors are three different types of neutrino" (physically wrong: e/mu/tau share
one nature, all Dirac or all Majorana; Dirac-vs-Majorana applies to the whole family, not per
flavor). The SURVIVING version:
  In a non-holomorphic modular construction (Qu-Ding type), each generation carries a DIFFERENT
  MODULAR WEIGHT (e.g. charged leptons k=1,3,5 in Qu-Ding eq.4.1). The weight fixes WHICH
  polyharmonic Maass form couples the generation, hence HOW MUCH non-holomorphic content
  (the y=Im(tau) part) enters. CONJECTURE: the generation hierarchy is correlated with this
  "dose of non-holomorphy" (the weight), not primarily with a mass put in by hand.

## Why this is only [CONJECTURE], not [DERIVATION]
- The eml-star judge answers "is this formula anti-holomorphic? yes/no" (a local Wirtinger test).
  It CANNOT answer "why three generations" -- that is one of the deepest open questions in
  physics and is NOT decidable by d/dzbar on a formula.
- No simulation in the current pipeline tests this. Claiming otherwise would be a false positive
  on the single most important question. There is NO test to run tonight.
- The Dirac/Majorana <-> mirror/chiral correspondence (suggested by the door images) is an
  ANALOGY, not a proven identity. "particle=antiparticle" resembles "anti = conj(holo)" but
  resemblance is not identity (Anthony's own rule: distrust seductive analogies). To promote it
  one would have to PROVE the Majorana condition maps to the judge's mirror verdict. Not done.

## What WOULD be needed to start testing it (cold, conceptual)
1. Settle the integer-vs-fractional weight obstacle (see FINDINGS_20260614_kY_convention_verdict)
   so an eclectic Delta(54) construction even has well-defined weights.
2. In Qu-Ding's existing T' model, check whether the WEIGHT assignment (1,3,5) is FORCED by data
   or chosen for fit -- i.e. is the "dose of non-holomorphy" per generation a prediction or an
   input? If forced -> the conjecture has a foothold. If chosen -> it is decoration.
3. Define what observable would distinguish "hierarchy from weight/geometry" vs "hierarchy from
   mass parameters". Without such an observable the conjecture is not falsifiable (false-precision
   rule: a distinction earns its place only if some claim depends on which side one is on).

## Status
[CONJECTURE] -- a well-posed research question, NOT a result. Honestly scoped: beautiful,
possibly deep, currently untestable by the eml-star judge alone. To be revisited cold, starting

-> Kirsch -> no-go -> CP -> Delta(54) -> Maass forms) is itself a validation of the method's
